Pizza Recipe

Ingredients :

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese (Mozarella, Cheddar, Gouda, or anything you'd like!)
  • Other Toppings (Bacon, Ham, Peppers, Hot Sauce, or anything you'd like!)

Steps :

  1. Make the pizza dough.
  2. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees fehrenheit.
  3. Prepare your toppings.
  4. Flatten your pizza dough.
  5. Spread your pizza sauce on the dough.
  6. Sprinkle your cheese on your pizza.
  7. Place any desired toppings onto your pizza.
  8. Place the pizza into the oven and bake for about 10-15 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven, let cooldown, and serve!
