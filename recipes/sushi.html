Sushi Recipe

Ingredients :

  • Seaweed (for sushi making, not snacking!)
  • Sushi Rice
  • Raw Fish (Salmon is good for beginners)
  • Other Fillings (Avocado, Cucumber, Cream Cheese, anything you'd like!)

Steps :

  1. Place your seaweed flat on your sushi mat.
  2. Spread the seaweed with a layer of prepared sushi rice.
  3. Cut your raw fish.
  4. Place your desired ingredients into the middle of your rice.
  5. Using your bamboo matt roll your sushi up as tight as possible.
  6. Last, use a sharp knife to cut your sushi roll into pieces!
