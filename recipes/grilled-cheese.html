Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients :

  • Unsalted Butter
  • Bread
  • Cheese (American, Mozarella, Cheddar, or anything you'd like!)
  • Other Toppings (Bacon, Ham, Peppers, Hot Sauce, or anything you'd like!)

Steps :

  1. Spread 1/2 Tbsp butter on one side of each piece of bread.
  2. Set a skillet over medium/low heat and place 2 slices of bread butter-side-down.
  3. On one piece of bread stack your cheese, then toppings.
  4. On the other piece of bread stack more cheese.
  5. Continue cooking until your bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, usually 5-6 minutes.
  6. Place one piece of bread on top of other, remove from skillet, and serve!
